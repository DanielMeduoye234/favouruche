# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Potential employers, small business owners, founders, and team leads who need help with social media operations and customer conversations. They are scanning quickly for trust, communication range, and evidence that Favour can organize public posts and private customer care without dropping detail.

## Product Purpose

This portfolio introduces Favour Uche-Gabriel as a social media manager and customer representative agent. Success means a visitor understands what she handles, sees how she thinks about campaigns and customer replies, and has a direct way to start a conversation.

## Brand Personality

Bright, composed, responsive. The voice should feel warm and precise: friendly enough for community work, organized enough for brand operations, and calm under customer pressure.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic influencer templates, overused purple gradients, beige personal-brand layouts, fake client logos, and vague buzzword copy. The site should not feel like a marketing agency homepage or a corporate support dashboard.

## Design Principles

Show the work behind the feed: make planning, inbox care, reporting, and brand voice visible.

Balance warmth with control: personality should come through, but the page must feel reliable.

Prefer concrete service language: name the actual tasks Favour can take off a team's plate.

Make contact low friction: the next step should be obvious without forcing visitors into a long funnel.

Design for quick scanning: recruiters and business owners should grasp her fit in under a minute.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, keyboard-friendly controls, visible focus states, semantic sections, reduced-motion support, and responsive layouts that keep text readable on mobile.
